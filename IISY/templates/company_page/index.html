Issue Tracking Made Easy

We enable end-users to report issues directly by scanning QR-codes, improving the workflow for maintenance employees and helping to resolve issues efficiently.

Solving the problem of issue reporting

A common problem among end-users is not knowing who to report issues to. The threshold for reporting may also be high, because it may require effort from the user. In the process of reporting the issue, the information may get lost.

Our goal is to streamline this process by allowing users to scan QR-codes on devices in order to report their status to maintenance. The status gets updated on the internal dashboard. This ensures that the status of all devices is known to maintenance at all times.
